Executive Summary

The Department of Defense Intellectual Property learning resources program demonstrates strong foundational capabilities with 67 resources across 7 audience types and 9 topic areas. Addressing these strategic gaps will strengthen the program's ability to prepare the acquisition workforce for evolving IP challenges in modern defense systems.
